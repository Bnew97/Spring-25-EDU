About Me

Hi! I'm Bradley, currently taking college classes for Photography and Astronomy. I'm a passionate photographer with a growing love for capturing the beauty of the night sky. I specialize in astrophotography, and I strive to bring the wonder of the cosmos into focus. When I'm not chasing astrophotography, I explore nature and abstract photography and I like to find the beauty in the unexpected perspectives. As I continue to learn and refine my craft, I hope to share my journey and inspire others to see the world (both near and far) in a new light.
