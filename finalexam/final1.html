What is Astrophotography?

Astrophotography is the practice of capturing images of the night sky, from bright, familiar objects like the moon and planets to more distant wonders like nebulae and galaxies. It combines technical skill with artistic vision, offering a unique way to explore and appreciate the cosmos. Whether you're documenting a lunar eclipse or showcasing the Milky Way over a quiet landscape, astrophotography allows you to create powerful visual connections between Earth and space.

Getting started is more accessible than many people expect. With just a basic camera, a tripod, and a clear night, beginners can take impressive wide-field shots of stars or even catch the Milky Way. From there, it’s easy to build on your skills and equipment at your own pace. As technology becomes more affordable and user-friendly, astrophotography has opened up to anyone with curiosity, a bit of patience, and a passion for looking up.
